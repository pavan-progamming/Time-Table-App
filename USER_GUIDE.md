Welcome to your new Timetable App! This guide will help you use the "Offline Mode" features.

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Launch the App**: Open the app on your phone or browser.
*   **Welcome**: You will see a "Welcome" screen.
*   **Get Started**: Simply enter your name and click **"Let's Go!"** to enter your personal offline dashboard.
    *   *Note: Your name is used to personalize your experience.*

## 2. The Dashboard üè†
The home screen gives you a quick overview of your day:
*   **Stats**: See total tasks, completion rate, and pending items.
*   **Next Up**: Shows your very next class or task based on the current time.
*   **Weekly Progress**: A bar chart showing how productive you've been this week.
*   **Quick Actions**: Large buttons to quickly add a new entry.

## 3. Managing Your Timetable üìÖ
Go to the **"My Timetable"** tab (Calendar icon) to manage your schedule.

### Adding a Task
1.  Click the **(+) Add Entry** button.
2.  **Subject**: Enter the name (e.g., "Math Class").
3.  **Day & Time**: Select the day of the week and start/end times.
4.  **Repeat**: Choose "Daily" if this happens every day, or "None" for a one-time task.
5.  **Priority**: Set to High (Red), Medium (Orange), or Low (Green).
6.  Click **Create Entry**.

### Editing or Deleting
*   **Edit**: Click the **Pencil icon** on any task card to change details.
*   **Delete**: Click the **Trash icon** to remove a task.
*   **Mark Done**: Click the **Checkmark circle** to cross off a task when finished.

*   **Dark Mode**: Tap the **Moon icon** in the top header to switch between Dark and Light themes.

## 5. Troubleshooting
*   **Data Missing?**: Data is saved to your specific device/browser. If you clear app data/cache, your tasks will be reset.
